How it works

Three modes. One sidebar.

Lumen reads what's on your screen and gives you the social context and language you need — without ever leaving the app you're in.

Message decoder
Lumen reads your emails and Slack messages, surfaces the subtext and emotional tone, and drafts three ready-to-send replies that sound warm, confident, and socially fluent.
Live overlay
Pinned to the edge of Gmail and Slack, Lumen's sidebar appears the moment a message lands. No switching tabs, no copy-pasting — just instant clarity, in context.
Meeting guidance
During Google Meet and Zoom calls, Lumen reads live captions and delivers real-time guidance: what's happening, the emotional undercurrent, and a specific thing you could say right now.
